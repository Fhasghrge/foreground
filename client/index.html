The concept of foregrounding comes from the visual arts, as opposed to background.
Foregrounding is a concept in literary studies concerning making a linguistic utterance (word,
clause, phrase, phoneme, etc.) stand out from the surrounding linguistic context, from given
literary traditions or from more general world knowledge.
